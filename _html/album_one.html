At The Hague University of Applied Sciences the course "Creativity & Design" teaches and trains us students to develop our own creativity and our drawing and sketching skills. Sketching is an important skill to enhance creativity and to cooperate with others in design processes. In the course Design & Creativity we familiarise ourrself with design skills such as exploring solution spaces and generating ideas in visual ways. We learn about the design processes (iteration, diverge/converge), and practice various related skills for ideation. We also learn different creative techniques and experiment with different visual techniques.
